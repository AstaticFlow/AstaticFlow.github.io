Pricing

Stock Build

This option is our stock hardware with prefinished shells. Even though the cost is low, the quality is set to the same standard as our higher options.

Custom Color

This option is our stock hardware, but with your personal color. The drums are sent to be painted by our custom color lab after consultation with you on the design.

Fully Custom Design

Our fully custom option included a consultation with our engineers who will work with you to design a set entirely to your specifications. It will be truly unique and one-of-a-kind.
